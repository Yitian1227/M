水相占卜
《水相占卜》是一場超脫現實的預言之旅。觀者凝神向水面並誠心詢問心中的疑惑，再將指尖輕觸水面，剎那間，粼粼波光間即刻浮現「YES」或「NO」的答案。命運的箴言便隨波影顯現。
  • -使用軟體 : TouchDesigner
  • -設計年分 : 2025
  • -製作團隊 : 施亦恬
  • -負責項目 : 程式設計、互動設計、視覺設計
  • -作品材質、設備 : 投影機、網路攝影機
"Ask water" is a prophetic journey beyond reality. The viewer focuses on the water and sincerely asks the doubts in his heart, then touches the water surface with his fingertips. In an instant, the answer "YES" or "NO" will appear in the shimmering waves. The motto of fate will appear with the waves.
當手指靠近時，粒子會聚集環繞，觸摸後則即時投影占卜結果，實現宛如魔法般的水面預言。
  • -Software used: TouchDesigner
  • -Design year: 2025
  • -Production team: Shi Yitian
  • -Projects in charge: Programming, interactive design, visual design
  • -Material and equipment used: Projector, webcam
