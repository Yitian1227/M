Quanda Wall

光達 互動牆
運用光達感測與Unity，打造一座通往童年回憶的互動電梯。觀眾可觸控按鈕，穿梭各個樓層。每層都有獨特互動，如幫派大星的搖搖馬投幣，或為辛普森家庭轉台。甚至還有搭電梯時的等待時光及配樂。
  • -使用軟體 : touchdesigner
  • -設計年分 : 2025
  • -製作團隊 : 施亦恬
  • -負責項目 : 程式設計、互動設計、視覺設計
  • -作品材質、設備 : 投影機、網路攝影機
Using LiDAR sensing and Unity, we created an interactive elevator that takes you back to childhood memories. Visitors can touch buttons to travel through the floors. Each floor has unique interactions, such as coin-operated rocking horses for Peppa Pig or rotating channels for The Simpsons.
  • -Software used: touchdesigner
  • -Design year: 2025
  • -Production team: Shi Yitian
  • -Projects in charge: Programming, interactive design, visual design
  • -Material and equipment used: Projector, webcam
