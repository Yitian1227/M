Market Weathering

股市天氣
或許 天氣有一天也不會是客觀的
在未來某一天淪為一種政治手段，也不無可能。

這項作品是一場交易
是老百姓和無形大手之間的拉鋸戰

使用者需要不斷投入硬幣來讓糟糕的天氣好轉
投入越多現金，越能換來雨過天晴
但過不了多久又會烏雲漫天....
天氣的波動如同股市
也是一種平民百姓與無形大手間的拉扯
若想讓波動產生變化，請以金錢來交換。
  • -使用軟體 : unity+arduino
  • -設計年分 : 2025
  • -製作團隊 : 施亦恬
  • -負責項目 : 程式設計、互動設計、視覺設計
  • -作品材質、設備 : 投影機、網路攝影機
Perhaps the weather will not be objective one day. It is possible that it will become a political means one day in the future. This work is a transaction. It is a tug-of-war between ordinary people and invisible big hands. Users need to keep putting coins in to make the bad weather better. The more cash you put in, the more likely it is to be sunny. But it won’t be long before the sky is full of dark clouds again. The fluctuation of weather is like the stock market. It is also a tug-of-war between ordinary people and invisible big hands. If you want to change the fluctuation, please exchange it with money.
